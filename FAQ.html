Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Muttamins products and services

Products & Ingredients

1. Are Muttamins products safe for all dog breeds?

Yes! Our supplements are formulated to be safe for all dog breeds and sizes. However, we always recommend consulting with your veterinarian before starting any new supplement regimen, especially if your dog has existing health conditions or is taking medications.

Ordering & Shipping

1. How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 5–7 business days to reach most locations. Delivery times may vary depending on your area and any unforeseen delays. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number to monitor its progress.

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Products must be unused and in their original packaging. Once we receive your return, we’ll process a full refund or exchange as requested.

Yes, we ship internationally to most countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. You will receive a tracking number once your order has been dispatched.

Usage & Dosage

1. How do I know which supplement my dog needs?

The best way to determine which supplement your dog needs is to consult your veterinarian. They can evaluate your dog’s health, age, breed, and diet to recommend the right supplement. Always follow professional guidance before starting any new supplement regimen.

2. Can I give my dog multiple supplements?

Yes, in many cases dogs can take multiple supplements, but it should be done carefully. Always consult your veterinarian to ensure the combination is safe and appropriate for your dog’s health. Follow the recommended dosages and guidance to avoid over-supplementing.

3. When will I see results?

Results can vary depending on your dog’s age, health, and the supplement used. Some improvements may be noticed within a few weeks, while others may take 1–2 months. Consistency and following the recommended dosage are key to seeing the best results.
