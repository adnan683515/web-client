Our Story

Scientifically-formulated supplements that support your dog's health, vitality, and happiness. Veterinarian-approved and made with love.

Who We Are?

Muttamins was born from a simple belief: our dogs deserve the same quality of wellness care that we give ourselves.

It Started With Max

When our founder’s golden retriever, Max, started showing signs of joint discomfort, she searched everywhere for a supplement that met her standards. What she found disappointed her—products filled with fillers, unclear sourcing, and questionable quality. So she decided to create her own. Working with veterinarians and nutritionists, she developed a line of supplements that she’d feel confident giving to Max. Today, Muttamins serves thousands of dogs across the country, each formula crafted with the same love and care she gave to Max.
